Gear Strength:

The calculated gear strength is expressed in terms of bending stress and surface durability. These calcuations are based on equations published by the Japanese Gear Manufacturer Association (JGMA). 

Specifications used:
JGMA 401-01	Bending strength formula of spur and helical gears
JGMA 402-01	Surface durability formula of spur and helical gears

These formulae apply to gears in the following range:

Module (m):	1.5 to 25mm
Pitch Diameter (d):	25 to 3200mm
Tangential Speed (v):	<25m/s
Rotating Speed (n):	<3600rpm

Refer to SDP/SI Metric Handbook Pgs T-151 to T-155 for help on determining the relevant factors. See sheet "Allowable Bending Stress Table" to lookup the allowable bending stress at the root for the chosen material.
